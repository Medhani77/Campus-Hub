AIESEC
Global Leadership, Exchange and Social Impact

AIESEC is the world's largest youth-led organisation, present in over 120 countries. At ORYN, the AIESEC chapter empowers students through leadership development programmes, global exchange opportunities, and social impact projects. Build your professional skills, travel the world, and become a global citizen making a real difference.

LEO Club
Community Service, Volunteering and Character Building

LEO Club ORYN is part of the worldwide Lions International organisation — one of the largest service club networks on the planet. We develop leadership, experience and opportunity in young people who volunteer to build stronger communities. Blood donation drives, community clean-up campaigns, charity fundraisers and much more — LEO members lead with purpose.

IEEE ORYN Student Branch connects engineering and technology students with the world's largest professional technical organisation. We run workshops on electronics, embedded systems, IoT, robotics and AI.
